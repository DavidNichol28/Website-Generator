# Destroying Techno-Feudalism

Rebuild Forge is a response the the ongoing economic dynamics summed up under the ideas of Techno-Feudalism, and is designed to supplant the stranglehold that Techno-Lords have on our Public Digital Infrastructure so that they lose their ability to withhold our access to software for their personal benefits.

## The Landscape
The **gate-keeping** of The Power of Software is done by the tech companies to everyone, but what power they don't stranglehold is then gate-kept **_by us, the developers_** because we want our high paying jobs.

### Why Must This Be?
They gatekeep everything from entertainment, to human interaction, to education, to tools of economic empowerment... **everything!**

They own the patents for **everything,** and everyone says that means its okay for them to hold software - **THE Miracle of Human Acheivement** - as *their property* that they can do *whatever they want with* - the fruits of our species's millenia of progress, including, but not limited to: 
1. Harvesting aspects of our previously unvalued psychological attributes that they now use to manipulate us, 
2. Taxing us for using literally anything, 
3. Actively working toward creating addiction/technical dependency in our naive, defenseless minds so we can never leave their *"platform,"* and therefore always provide blood for them to drink.


### Privatized Digital Infrastructure
We don't let Zuck own our roads or our skies, right, so why does he own the world's ability to communicate?

Is this *really* what we **_want?_**

The time comes where the monopolists that birthed the next stage of technology are removed from their vampiric relationship to said new technology.

**_Every single aspect_** of our usage of computers **require** the use of a *"software platform"* that exists for no reason but to **maintain control** over what is now a  *vital part of all aspects of social technologies* - including tech as *prehistoric and vital* as **politics, education, communication,** and even **_culture, itself!_** 

**_"OMG! Did you see the new Matrix money-grab on Netflix??? We HAVE TO talk about it for the next week until a different company bombards me with different ads to tell me what to talk about!"_**

## Taking Action
### Evaluating Realistic Options
Now, I have ideas about taking down the super-giants, but addressing a network is maximum-difficulty, so I'm chipping away at the lower-level gate-keepers that charge us for ${"Digital Infrastructure"work_title:Blog Digital Infrastructure}$ that'll be easier to replace.

That being said, how do we bring this about? 

Who will help us..?

Unfortunately, due to the current and foreseeable future of world politics, it is painfully clear that the *power* of the Techo-Lords **_will never be tempered in our favor_** if we *depend on the governments.*

I won't waste time expecting the *world's most believed liars* to bite the hand that *actually* feeds them, in favor of what they value as their *worthless terrorist-attack-fodder.* 

#### The People!
We, developers, have more than everything we need to build a ${"Free, Open, and Accessible Public Digital Infrastructure"work_title:Blog Free, Open, and Accessible Digital Public Works}$ which would be run for and by the people. This does not exist due to the unbelievably wasteful incentive structure that exists for software companies: the more restrictive they make access, the more potential to make money (Google/Apple "app marketplaces" being the most apalling examples).

Developers have to be okay with destroying their insanely high, artificially inflated salaries for an abstract ideal of *"the future of the world,"* and that's a ton to ask of people trying to survive in a world that doesn't care, but its gone on for far too long, and the price to pay only gets worse the longer we draw this out. 

Also, *we're all out of jobs now,* so it **angry-mob time.**

#### How to Fight?
The way to fight it is simple:

###### We stop building to be *"good enough"* for some meaningless, soul-sucking job to grant us some relief from poorness, and use our efforts for the betterment of the whole of society and its future.

### What Are We Building?
The focus is on **destroying companies** that produce *boatloads of money* by **_extorting us for software._**

Its bad enough that there's already no chance of beating a big company - only getting bought by one as the pinacle of modern business success - but they suck small business owners dry before they're even successful.
