# All The Software For Everyone, Forever

Nothing is more empowering than **access to software.**

Software is how we turn *computers into tools*, and computers are how we *amplify human intelligence.* The earlier and the wider software is available, the more people can use machines to radically expand and enhance their intelligence to solve real problems, build what they and their community need, and live a better life.

The ability to receive, transfer, create, and validate information with accuracy and precision isn’t an academic or technical skill—it’s the foundation of modern power. Every industry, every movement, every innovation depends on it.

Even modern and modernized *hobbies* require it.

So why is something this fundamental still locked away?

It is a **global social imperative** to get **_all the software into all the hands_** in a way that is *actually useful to non-engineers.*

If you want to help bring the power of software to The People, then help us do more in whichever way(s) you can.
