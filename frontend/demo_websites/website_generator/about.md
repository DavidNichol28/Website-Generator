# Web Development Is A Scam
---
Are we really gonna pay for this ancient (and garbage) tech service forever?

Websites are ancient tech.

Stop being a part of the problem.

## The Problem We're Solving
The website industry has made it such that people need a lot of knowledge, skill, and effort to get a website running, or be willing to pay a megacorp money to have the same hyper-consumerist flashbang of stock images of people smiling miserably and data-farming popups.

Combine this with the diabolical trap that is the ease-of-setup and absolute technical nightmare that one of those websites become once you start doing stuff with it...

## Our Solution
People need to write out the stuff they need to write in order to have anything to put on the website, right?

So, if someone were to **actually care** about the **user** and nobody else... 

How would they go about making it as simple as possible to take that writing and transform it into a website?

We take that writing, as it is, ask that it be written in MarkDown (or our ExtendedMarkDown which is MD with stuff for a website that MD doesn't have), and turn it straight into a website. The pages and navigation is all handled programmatically crawling to see how the writing is set up in the directory that is its indicated root.

In English: We turn that writing straight into a fully functional, nested navigation (if they want) website.
